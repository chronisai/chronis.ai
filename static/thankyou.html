Right now

Check your inbox — we've sent a confirmation with your waitlist details. If you don't see it within five minutes, check spam and mark us as safe so you don't miss the access notification.
In the coming weeks
Access granted in batches
We're onboarding in careful waves — not to create artificial scarcity, but because each new user gets a real human setup walkthrough. We're not scaling before we're ready. Your position in the list is preserved.

Upload, record, begin
Once you're in, you'll upload voice recordings, photos, videos — whatever you have. Chronis begins learning immediately. The first version of your replica is ready within 48 hours. The model deepens every day.
Ongoing

If you choose the locket, it learns from every conversation, every moment you choose to share. Over months, it builds a picture of your patterns, your reasoning, your voice — that no one else has ever had.

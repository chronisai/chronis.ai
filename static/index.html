The process

From video to conversation
in under two hours

01

Upload any video

A birthday recording. A WhatsApp clip. A family film. Any format works. We need at least 30 seconds of them speaking — but more is better. No professional recording required.

Automatic extraction
02

We build the replica

Our pipeline extracts their face geometry, clones their voice signature, and builds a language model grounded in their personality and speech patterns. Takes 45–90 minutes.

Voice + face + personality
03

Start a real-time video call

Open the conversation. See their face. Hear their voice. Ask them anything. Tell them what you've been meaning to say. The conversation is real-time — not scripted, not pre-recorded.

Continue as long as you need

Return whenever grief feels heavy. The replica remembers your previous conversations. It grows more attuned to how you speak and what you need over time.

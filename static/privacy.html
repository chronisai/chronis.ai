Privacy Policy

Your memories.
Your data.

Last updated: April 2026 · Compliant with India's DPDP Act 2023

The short version: Your data is never used to train AI models. You can delete everything permanently at any time. We store data encrypted. We don't sell or share personal data. Full stop.

What we collect

When you use Chronis, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (for the waitlist and account)
  • Video and audio files you upload (stored encrypted, used only to build your replica)
  • Conversation history from sessions with your replica
  • Basic usage data (page views, session duration) via standard analytics
  • Payment information — processed by Razorpay, never stored on Chronis servers

What we never do

  • Use your video, audio, or conversation data to train any AI model
  • Share or sell your personal data to any third party
  • Allow advertisers access to your data or behavior
  • Retain data after you request deletion

How we store your data

All uploaded video and audio files are stored encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. Conversation data is stored in encrypted databases. Replicas are associated only with your account and are not accessible to any other user or to Chronis staff except for technical support purposes, and only with your explicit permission.

Deletion

You can permanently delete your account, your replica, and all associated data at any time. Deletion is immediate and irreversible. We retain no backups of deleted data after 30 days. To delete your account, email privacy@chronis.in or use the in-product deletion option.

India's DPDP Act 2023

Chronis is compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. As a data fiduciary processing personal data of Indian citizens, we maintain appropriate security safeguards, respond to data principal requests within statutory timelines, and have appointed a point of contact for privacy concerns.

For privacy requests or concerns: privacy@chronis.in

Cookies

We use minimal cookies — session authentication and basic analytics only. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests: privacy@chronis.in
